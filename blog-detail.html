Understanding the Importance of Vitamins

Vitamins play a crucial role in your child's growth and development. They are essential nutrients that support various bodily functions, from bone development to immune system strength.

Key Takeaways

  • Vitamin D is crucial for bone development
  • Vitamin C supports immune system function
  • Iron is essential for cognitive development

Essential Vitamins for Children

Here are the most important vitamins your child needs for healthy development:

Vitamin D

Essential for calcium absorption and bone development.
